Percy Tears is a genius masterpiece divided in 6 episodes.

Release date: 05 mars 2030

Regi: Walter Soderlund

Actors: Robert Gustafsson, Jonas Inde, Johan Rheborg

Story: Percy Nilegaard has gone insane and goes to a shrink called Dr J. Tull. He tells the doctor the story of how he took over another mans life, Janne Fuglesang's. At the same time in Norrland, Kapten Klaenning is making his team of aeronauts ready to take a trip into space, and put the first Swede on the moon.
